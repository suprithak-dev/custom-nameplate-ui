Made in IndiaPremium-grade materials, crafted locally.

The PrakulArts SS Matte Nameplate is precision-cut from 304-grade stainless steel — the same grade used in commercial architecture. Each plate is sandblasted to a silky, uniform matte finish and laser-engraved with your custom text, creating a deep, permanent mark that never fades.

Designed to withstand Indian outdoor conditions — monsoons, coastal humidity, summer heat. Ships with stainless steel screws, wall plugs, and a protective peel film. A digital design proof is sent within 24 hours of ordering.

Installation: Drill 6 mm holes at the marked positions, insert wall plugs, fix with the included SS screws. Takes under 5 minutes with a basic drill.

Cleaning: Wipe with a damp soft cloth monthly. Mild soap is safe. Avoid abrasive scrubbers. The matte finish is fingerprint-resistant and stays clean with minimal effort — even outdoors.

Can I get a preview before payment?We send the design proof after order placement, before manufacturing. You must approve the design before we start crafting.
Can I include a logo or symbol?Yes! Mention it in the Special Instructions field. We support custom logos, religious symbols (Om, Cross, etc.), and decorative elements.
What languages are supported?English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, and most Indian scripts. Specify your language when ordering.
What if I don't like the design?You can request unlimited revisions on the digital proof. We only manufacture once you're 100% happy.
